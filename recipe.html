Spaghetti and Meatballs Recipe

This recipe will help you cook a delicous spaghetti meat with meatballs.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground pork sausage
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup basil
  • 1 cup parsley
  • 2 eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • 1 box of spaghetti noodles
  • 3 cans crushed tomatoes
  • Sea salt
  • Red chili pepper flakes
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Make meatballs by combining ground meat, half of the basil and parsley, eggs, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
  2. Bake meatballs in a 400 degree oven for 25 min.
  3. Heat olive oil in pot. Saute onions with garlic. Add canned tomatoes and tomato paste. Add remaining basil and parsley. Simmer for 15 min.
  4. When sauce has thickened, season to taste with salt. Add meatballs and simmer for 30 min.
  5. Prepare spaghetti by following instructions on box.
  6. Once spaghetti is ready, serve immediately. Enjoy!
